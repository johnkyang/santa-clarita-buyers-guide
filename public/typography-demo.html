Understanding Earnest Money Deposits in Santa Clarita

In a competitive market like Valencia or Stevenson Ranch, the size of your earnest money deposit can influence whether a seller accepts your offer. Most Santa Clarita transactions see deposits between 1–3% of the purchase price — but in multiple-offer situations, going to 3% signals serious intent.

Your earnest money is held in escrow and fully credited toward your down payment at closing. It's only at risk if you remove contingencies and then walk away — something your buyer's agent should help you avoid.

  • Standard deposit range: 1–3% of purchase price
  • Competitive offer deposit: 3–5% to stand out
  • Timeline: due within 3 business days of acceptance
  • Protected by contingencies until you remove them
